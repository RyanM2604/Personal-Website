  • Second Chance

    I also lead Second Chance, a remarkable social enterprise on a mission to create Second Chances for the incredible people of Klong Toey, Bangkok's largest slum. We're dedicated to making a difference through the power of Re(up)cycling, Reusing, and Renewing. Our team is passionate about transforming discarded items into opportunities, and we believe that every piece has the potential for a second life, just like the individuals in our community. Through innovative initiatives, we're working to uplift lives, promote sustainability, and inspire positive change in Klong Toey. Together, we're turning what some might see as waste into precious chances for a better future.
